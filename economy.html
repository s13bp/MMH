Build a realm for free, or buy land in the metropolis to promote and drive traffic to your realm

Land in Realm

The Metropolis is the central hub where players enter Realm’s metaverse. It is a place to socialize, find cool Realms and discover quests!

There are four tiers of land in the Metropilis, the most valuable of which is at the center with the most footfall.

All lands can be customized and branded; they contain a Portal which drives footfall to a realm.

Realms can be infinitely scalable and you can connect them together to create your own Microverse or let customers/fans build houses in your Microverse.
