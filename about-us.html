Blockchain technology is a decentralized and secure system for recording and verifying transactions. It uses a distributed ledger to keep track of data, which is stored on a network of computers. This makes it highly resistant to fraud and hacking, as the data is not stored in a central location that can be easily manipulated. Blockchain technology is used in various industries, including finance, healthcare, and supply chain management, and has the potential to revolutionize the way we conduct transactions and share information.
